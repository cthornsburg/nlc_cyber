# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the Contributor Covenant.

## Our pledge

We want a community that is welcoming, professional, and focused on learning.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on the work and the learning outcome.
- Keep discussions safe for students and community members.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Sharing doxxing information or private student information.
- Encouraging unsafe or illegal activity.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact:
- ithornsburg@alamo.edu

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to keep the community safe and productive.
